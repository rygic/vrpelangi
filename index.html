Sidoarjo Legends

Explore the history and myths of Sidoarjo Regency's origins through Virtual Reality technology.

Origin of Sidoarjo Name

The name Sidoarjo comes from the words "Sido" meaning "to become" and "Arjo" meaning "valuable". This name was reportedly given by a regent who hoped this area would become a prosperous and valuable region.

Legend of Joko Bayu

The story of Joko Bayu and his struggle against Dutch colonists in the Sidoarjo region. Joko Bayu is known as a local hero who persistently defended his homeland.

Water Source Myth

Legend of a magical water source that gives fertility to the land of Sidoarjo. This water source was reportedly discovered by a hermit who was meditating in this area.
